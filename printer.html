PRINTERS

LASER AND INKJET

An inkjet printer prints images or text by pushing droplets of liquid ink onto paper or other surfaces. These printers are famous for their ability to produce high-quality colour prints and are widely used at home and in the office.



PLOTTER

A plotter is a specialised output device that produces large-scale, high-quality printouts and drawings. Unlike traditional printers, plotters use pens or markers to draw continuous lines on paper or other materials, making them ideal for creating complex technical drawings, maps, and architectural plans.

XEROX MACHINE

Xerox machines are widely used in offices, businesses, and educational institutions to duplicate documents, reports, and other printed materials. They come in various sizes and functionalities, ranging from compact desktop models to larger, multifunctional copiers that can also scan, fax, and print.
