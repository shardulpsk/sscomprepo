INFRARED

Infrared security cameras can catch footage in both low and no light conditions.


DAY NIGHT

A day and night camera includes modified lenses that allow for infrared emission produced by infrared LEDs.

BULLET

Bullet cameras are a form of security camera meant to be tiny, cylindrical, and simple to install.

PTZ

PAN-TILT-ZOOM Cameras are designed with mechanical components that allow them to swivel left and right, tilt up and down, and zoom in and out of scenes.


DOME

Dome-shaped surveillance cameras used as part of a business security camera system.




OUT DOOR

Outdoor cameras are constructed with durable and weather-resistant materials.They have a sealed enclosure to protect the sensitive electronics within from dust and other environmental factors.
